What is this odd link?

That link is a sort-of tunnel for your connection to go from YOUR device to the URL which forwards all connections to a port where my device returns the HTML output for YOUR device to display. Think of it like a shipping port where things go in and out like a trade.

In short: Your Device --[a request to open the site]--> URL --[a request to open the site]--> My Device

Then: My Device --[the site content]--> URL --[the site content]--> Your Device --[displays the content]--> Your Browser

What is this site for?

Mostly just experimenting.

Although, a model like this is similar to large-scale web servers, so it is good practice for me.
